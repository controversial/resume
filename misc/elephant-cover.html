Dear Elephant,

I’m very excited to learn of the opportunity for internship positions at Elephant. I’ve been working at a small digital studio in the Hudson Valley for the past two summers helping to craft compelling digital experiences for a variety of clients. I find Elephant’s focus on the human experience—combining UX and brand experience with a larger cultural experience—to be fascinating and deeply compelling.

My personal focus has been on the intersection of design and technology, and I engage much more readily with creative problems than purely technical ones. I view software as a platform for creative expression that combines design, technology, and human experience. I’ve been building websites and web apps for five years now, during which time I’ve mastered JavaScript, CSS, HTML, React, and Vue. While at first, I started out by building simple static sites using prebuilt component libraries, my skillset has followed my passion, and I’ve gained expertise in building interactive web experiences from the ground up. Today, my favorite projects to work on are those driven by rich animation, thoughtful responsive design, and complex functionality. I’ve loved the process of learning along the way more than anything else. Expanding my technical knowledge has always felt exciting because it is empowering; expanding my toolset enables me to implement ideas that were previously out of reach, thereby unlocking entire new sectors of possibility.

One project I’d like to bring to your attention is Sleep Later (sleeplater.now.sh), an e-commerce web app that I designed and developed as a freelance project for a local fashion brand. I started by creating a brand identity, and then developed a unified digital experience that reinforces that identity consistently as customers move from browsing through products all the way to checkout. I designed the entire site in Sketch, and built the whole experience by hand in Vue, which I learned as I went. I also developed an Airtable integration that allowed the client to track and update inventory and orders.

Working at Elephant would give me an opportunity to build on this experience and create similar product experiences for some of the brands I most admire and reach worldwide audiences. Given my skills, experience and passions, I’m confident that I could value to the Elephant team as either a Creative Technology Intern or an Engineering Intern. I’m excited by the work Elephant has done as well as its mission, and I’d love the opportunity to spend another summer working to bring engaging and beautiful experiences to life.

Thanks in advance for your consideration.

Regards,
Luke Deen Taylor
